    Imposter Syndrome @ Amazon

    In this article, an Amazon employee confessed how he shouldn’t be hired at Amazon in the first place. He lacked a lot of technical and leadership skills. But over the period of 2 years, he grew himself to be a worthy SDE-2. He did it by collecting constructive feedback from his peers and reflecting on them. He did a promotion-driven career development. He asked regular feedback from his manager and collected datapoints and written feedback to grow over the period of time.

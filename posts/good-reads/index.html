Collecting good articles that I found over the period of time and small summaries about them.

Imposter Syndrome @ Amazon

In this article, an Amazon employee confessed how he shouldn’t be hired at Amazon in the first place. He lacked a lot of technical and leadership skills. But over the period of 2 years, he grew himself to be a worthy SDE-2. He did it by collecting constructive feedback from his peers and reflecting on them. He did a promotion-driven career development. He asked regular feedback from his manager and collected datapoints and written feedback to grow over the period of time.

  • Link - https://medium.com/@curtiseinsmann/amazon-shouldnt-have-hired-me-here-s-how-i-became-an-sde2-e310d0d264a8

Managing Covid Risk

Really cool article, where a person created a process to track his Covid Risk when outside based on surrounding’s precautionary standard. Give a read and take notes, it can be used in other places and use to model real-life.

  • Link - http://philipmw.github.io/2020/08/10/covid-risk-tracking.html

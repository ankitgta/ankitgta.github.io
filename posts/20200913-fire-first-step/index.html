FIRE yourself

What is FIRE

Let’s first start by defining FIRE. It is short for “Financial Independence Retire Early”. This is sort of an internet movement where people save until their very last penny. Invest that money wisely in a retirement account. Constantly track their progress and growth towards their goals. And when it is time, they say FU to their bosses and FIRE themselves from their job.

Who it is for

It is for anyone who has a day-job and wants to not be in the same place for their entire life. It doesn’t require anyone to have a high-income job. Even a pizza delivery guy can achieve FIRE. Having said that the path and goals differ a lot. People use terms like lean-FIRE or fat-FIRE to imply how lavishly you want to spend your retirement. A trip to Bahamas or to your local zoo is all the difference to having a Fat vs a Lean-FIRE.

Why FIRE

Why would someone want to this? There could be a variety of reasons. For me, it is about not being stuck at the same job till I die. My job is high stress and sometimes less rewarding. It requires me to constantly upgrade my skills, survive in a competitive environment. Not that I don’t like it or anything but I don’t see myself doing this for next 15 years or so. I want to take the first chance I can get to get out of this racket. Or maybe switch to a role that is not as demanding as the current one.
